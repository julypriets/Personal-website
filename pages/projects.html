General Motors App

The app was intended to improve the user experience of costumers at General Motors dealerships. Users would scan a QR code and receive all the important information about the vehicle. They would also chat with an AI so they could ask any details about the vehicles.

The desktop version of the app allowed the administrators of the different dealerships to see the demographic information of their clients, what where their preferences and also a real time dashboard to track the questions users made to the AI so they could take care of them personally if needed.


Since this project is not available for the public yet, there is no demo available .

Atpos - Software Architecture

I made this project with my teammates from the Sprinters Team. We had to design the architecture for a software that allowed electronic payments within small stores and sent its billing to the DIAN.
